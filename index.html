Trip to San Diego, California
San Diego, California

Balboa Park

I visited Balboa park also dubbed as "the cultural heart of San Diego". Balboa park is located less than 20 minutes from downtown. Balboa park hosts the San Diego zoo, 18 museums as well as beautiful gardens.

La Jolla Shores

After our visit to Balboa park, we ate a local Mexican restaurant and then rested. The following day we visited La Jolla Shores. You have not visited San Diego if you have not stepped foot on any of the beautiful San Diego beaches. La Jolla Shores is a sandy beach approximately 1 mile long located in La Jolla, California. The sunset was breathtaking coupled with the sound of the waves and the breeze from the ocean. It was a very relaxing moment for my family.

Sea World

We ended our San Diego vacation by visiting Sea World. Sea World San Diego is an animal theme park, oceanarium, outside aquarium, and marine mammal park, in San Diego, California, United States, inside Mission Bay Park. My son especially enjoyed his time watching killer whales.
